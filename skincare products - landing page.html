My Routine
AM:
  • Step 1: Rinse face with only lukewarm water.
  • Step 2: Pat water into face with hands. Do not towel dry. (Face needs to be damp/wet.)
  • Step 3: Pour two drops of Rovectin Activating Treatment Lotion into palm and apply to face. Pat into face until absorbed into skin.
  • Step 4: Squirt out a pump of GOODAL Green Tangerine Vitamin C Serum and apply evenly to face. Let it absorb into skin.
  • Step 5: Apply a nickel-sized amount of Beauty of Joseon Relief Sun : Rice + Probiotics (SPF50+ PA++++) to skin.
PM:
  • Step 1: On dry face, apply a pump of Manyo Pure Cleansing Oil to face and massage for a minute to remove makeup and declog pores.
  • Step 2: Emulsify the cleansing oil by wetting palms and massaging the water into the cleansing oil for 30 seconds.
  • Step 3: Rinse off the emulsified cleansing oil and double-cleanse with Kiehl's Ultra Facial Cleanser.
  • Step 4: Pat face with towel until semi-dry. Skin should still be damp.
  • Step 5: Apply two drops of Rovectin Activating Treatment Lotion to skin and pat in until absorbed.
  • Step 6: Apply Good Molecules Discoloration Correcting Serum to skin and wait until it is fully absorbed.
  • Step 7: Apply The Ordinary Natural Moisturizing Factors + HA to skin.
  • Step 8: Brush on RapidLash Eyelash Enhancing Serum on lashline and lashes and brows.
